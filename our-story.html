Our Story

Hi, I’m the founder of Lumos Glow. 🌸

I started this journey with a simple dream — 100 days, 1 lakh rupees challenge. It sounded impossible. Every day felt harder, but I refused to give up. I poured my heart into every candle, believing that each flame would carry my story of struggle, hope, and courage.

This brand isn’t just about candles. It’s about luxury, love, and persistence. Every piece is handcrafted, not just to brighten rooms, but to warm souls.

Today, Lumos Glow is more than a candle company. It’s a branded luxury experience ✨. And you, by supporting this dream, are part of my story.

Thank you for believing in me. Thank you for lighting this journey. 🕯️
